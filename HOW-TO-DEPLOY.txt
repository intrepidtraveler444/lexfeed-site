LEXFEED — How to put it online (free) and use it on your phone
================================================================

EASIEST METHOD — drag & drop (no account signup needed beyond Netlify)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Go to:  https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag this WHOLE folder (lexfeed-site) onto the page.
3. Wait ~10 seconds. Netlify gives you a live URL like:
      https://random-name-12345.netlify.app
4. Open that URL on your phone's browser.

PUT IT ON YOUR HOME SCREEN (so it opens like an app)
----------------------------------------------------
iPhone (Safari):
   - Open the URL in Safari
   - Tap the Share button (square with up-arrow)
   - Tap "Add to Home Screen" -> Add
Android (Chrome):
   - Open the URL in Chrome
   - Tap the three-dot menu
   - Tap "Add to Home screen" / "Install app"

It will get the LexFeed scales icon and open full-screen with no
browser bars — just like a native app.

RENAME YOUR SITE (optional)
---------------------------
In Netlify: Site settings -> Change site name
   e.g. https://lexfeed-uk.netlify.app

UPDATING LATER
--------------
When you change index.html, just drag the folder onto
https://app.netlify.com/drop again (or, if you made an account,
go to your site's Deploys tab and drag it there to redeploy).

WHAT'S IN THIS FOLDER
---------------------
  index.html            <- the app (this is what loads)
  manifest.webmanifest  <- makes it installable as an app
  icon.svg              <- the home-screen icon
  netlify.toml          <- tells Netlify how to serve it
  HOW-TO-DEPLOY.txt     <- this file

NOTE: Hosting on Netlify (https://) instead of opening the file
directly also makes the live news/case/article feeds load more
reliably than from your computer.
